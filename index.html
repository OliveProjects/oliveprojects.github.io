Professionally curious.

Curiosity is the common thread. By day I enforce environmental law in Sweden, by night I'm building apps, configuring flight controllers or watching stock charts. Different worlds, same drive: understanding how things work and making them better.

Olive Projects is where the rest happens. Retail investor since 2017, vibe coder since early 2026. Everything here is built out of curiosity.
