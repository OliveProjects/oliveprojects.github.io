Business Model Canvas

Premium NFC Business Cards — Sweden, Scandinavia & France  |  Updated June 2026

Key Partners

  • Tom / ifPrint.eu — European printing & manufacturing partner; provides card production expertise, quality standards, and established processes
  • Frank — Owner of the card production facility in China; handles large-scale manufacturing of metal, wood, and PVC cards at lower cost
  • Shipping carriers — PostNord, Bring, and/or DHL for delivery within Sweden and Scandinavia; Chronopost/DHL for France
  • Payment processor — Stripe, Klarna or similar for invoicing and online payments
  • NFC chip supplier — Provider of NFC inlays (embedded in metal/wood/PVC cards)
  • Bahnhof (Sweden) — VPS Stockholm; hosts the vCard platform at 100 kr/mån; Swedish provider, GDPR-compliant, no third-party dependencies

Key Activities

  • Sales & customer acquisition (LinkedIn, Google Ads, cold outreach)
  • Customer service & order management (handled by the owners)
  • Design consultation & artwork preparation for each order
  • Coordinating production between Frank (manufacturing) and Tom (print quality)
  • NFC programming & setup for digital cards
  • Shipping & order fulfillment to Scandinavia and France
  • Hosting & maintaining digital vCard pages for NFC customers
  • Automated customer onboarding — welcome email with login credentials sent via Resend when a new customer is created

Key Resources

  • Frank's manufacturing capacity in China (metal, wood, PVC)
  • Tom's printing expertise and quality process at ifPrint.eu
  • The owners' language skills (Swedish, French) and local market knowledge
  • Website with quote request system
  • Design software (Adobe Illustrator / Canva Pro)
  • vCard hosting platform — live at vcard.oliveprojects.eu (Node.js, PostgreSQL, Bahnhof VPS Stockholm)
  • Startup capital (est. 25,000 kr) for marketing and first stock

Value Proposition

  • Premium physical cards in metal (steel/aluminium), wood, and PVC — materials that make a lasting first impression
  • NFC-enabled — tap the card to instantly share contact details, LinkedIn profile, or website
  • Local service in Scandinavian languages — a gap ifPrint.eu doesn't fill (German-language only)
  • French-language service for the French market — another underserved gap
  • Design check included with every order — no extra cost
  • Fast quote response (within 24 hours)
  • Sustainable option — FSC-certified wood cards for eco-conscious clients
  • Digital vCard hosting — optional subscription so NFC cards always link to up-to-date contact info
  • Swiss-standard quality through Tom / ifPrint partnership

Customer Relationships

  • Personal, high-touch service via email and phone (the owners handle all contact)
  • Free design consultation included with each order
  • 24-hour quote response guarantee
  • Proactive follow-up after delivery to ensure satisfaction
  • Long-term account relationships for corporate clients (reorders, staff card updates)
  • Self-service digital vCard management portal for NFC hosting subscribers

Channels

  • Company website (quote request form, product showcase, gallery)
  • Google Ads (search ads targeting "visitkort metall", "NFC visitkort" etc.)
  • LinkedIn — organic content and outreach to B2B decision-makers
  • Instagram — visual showcase of finished card designs
  • Direct / cold outreach to hotels, agencies, and law firms
  • Word of mouth & referrals from satisfied clients
  • Trade fairs and networking events (longer term)

Customer Segments

  • Hotels & hospitality — staff cards, key cards, NFC check-in / room info
  • Corporate professionals — executives, sales teams, consultants wanting premium brand representation
  • Real estate agents & brokers — high card usage, brand-conscious
  • Law firms & financial advisors — premium image is essential
  • Politicians & public figures — personal branding, NFC to campaign site
  • Marketing & creative agencies — differentiated client gifts and self-promotion
  • Private individuals — entrepreneurs, freelancers, networkers

Primary markets: Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Finland. Secondary: France.

Cost Structure

  • Production costs — per-card manufacturing fee to Frank (China) or Tom (ifPrint); varies by material and quantity
  • Shipping costs — inbound (China/Europe → Sweden) and outbound (to customers); PostNord/DHL rates
  • Marketing — Google Ads budget, LinkedIn Ads (est. 25,000 kr startup allocation)
  • Website / domain — domain registration (~200–400 kr/year)
  • vCard hosting infrastructure — Bahnhof VPS Stockholm: 100 kr/mån = 1,200 kr/år fixed cost regardless of number of customers (capacity for ~100–200 customers on current plan); scales to ~2,400 kr/yr at 200+ customers
  • Design tools — Adobe Creative Cloud or similar (~3,000 kr/year)
  • Payment processing fees — ~1.5–2.5% per transaction (Stripe/Klarna)
  • Administrative & accounting — bookkeeping software (Fortnox or similar)
  • NFC chips — cost of embedding NFC inlays per card

Revenue Streams

  • Card sales — primary revenue; priced per unit with quantity discounts (e.g. from ~80 kr/card at low qty, down to ~50 kr/card at 100+)
  • NFC setup fee — one-time fee for programming and linking NFC chip to digital profile
  • vCard hosting subscription — annual recurring fee: Bas 99 kr/år (redigerbar profil) · Standard 119 kr/år (skrivskyddad, bevarad profil). Year 1 ingår gratis i kortpriset; Year 2+ near-pure margin. Driftkostnad: ~100 kr/mån oavsett antal kunder — jämnt upp vid 2 betalande prenumeranter. Plattform live: vcard.oliveprojects.eu (Bahnhof VPS Stockholm, Node.js/PostgreSQL)
  • Design/artwork fee — optional premium design service for clients without ready artwork
  • Corporate accounts — volume contracts with hotels or companies for annual card supply
  • Reorders — returning customers updating designs or ordering for new staff
