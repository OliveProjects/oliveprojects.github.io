Lean Canvas

Premium NFC Business Cards — Sweden, Scandinavia & France  |  Updated June 2026

Problem

Top 3 problems to solve

  • Paper business cards leave no lasting impression — they are discarded within days
  • Digital-only sharing (QR codes, AirDrop) lacks the tactile premium feel expected in high-end B2B contexts
  • No local Scandinavian- or French-language supplier offers premium NFC-enabled physical cards with personal service

Existing Alternatives

  • Standard paper card printers (Vistaprint, Canva Print, local print shops)
  • ifPrint.eu — Swiss quality, but German-only interface and no local support
  • Direct Chinese imports — low cost, but no quality control or local service
  • US NFC card brands (Linq, Popl) — not marketed to Scandinavia/France, no local language

Solution

  • Premium metal, wood, and PVC business cards with embedded NFC chip — tap to share contact info instantly
  • Full service in Swedish and French: quote within 24 h, design check included, personal follow-up
  • Optional digital vCard hosting so the card always links to up-to-date information

Key Metrics

  • Orders per month
  • Average order value (kr)
  • Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
  • NFC hosting subscription count & churn rate
  • Repeat / reorder rate
  • Quote-to-order conversion rate

Unique Value Proposition

Premium NFC business cards — in your language, with your design — that make a lasting first impression and work with a single tap.

  • Metal, wood, and PVC — materials that signal quality before a word is spoken
  • Local service in Swedish and French, something no competitor offers
  • Swiss-standard print quality via ifPrint.eu partnership
  • FSC-certified wood cards for sustainability-conscious clients

High-level Concept

"The premium business card that works like a smartphone — built for Scandinavian and French professionals."

Unfair Advantage

Hard to copy or buy

  • Language advantage — native Swedish + French service; no competitor covers both markets locally
  • Partner network — exclusive access to Frank (low-cost Chinese manufacturing) and Tom/ifPrint (Swiss quality process)
  • Personal owner-operated service — faster decisions and deeper client relationships than any agency
  • First-mover position in Scandinavian premium NFC card market

Channels

  • Google Ads (search: "visitkort metall", "NFC visitkort", "carte de visite métal")
  • LinkedIn — B2B organic content and direct outreach to decision-makers
  • Instagram — visual portfolio of finished card designs
  • Cold outreach to hotels, law firms, agencies
  • Word of mouth and client referrals

Customer Segments

  • Hotels & hospitality — staff cards, key cards, NFC for room info
  • Corporate professionals — executives, consultants, sales teams
  • Real estate agents — high card volume, brand-conscious
  • Law firms & financial advisors — premium image essential
  • Marketing & creative agencies — differentiated client gifts
  • Private individuals — entrepreneurs, freelancers

Early Adopters

  • Boutique hotels wanting to impress guests at check-in
  • Consultants and executives who attend frequent networking events

Cost Structure

  • Card manufacturing — per-unit cost to Frank (China) or Tom (ifPrint); varies by material and order size
  • Inbound shipping — China/Europe → Sweden (consolidation shipments)
  • Outbound shipping — PostNord/DHL/Bring to customers across Scandinavia; Chronopost for France
  • Marketing spend — Google Ads and LinkedIn Ads budget (est. 25,000 kr startup allocation)
  • Website & hosting — domain, WordPress/Webflow, NFC vCard platform (~1,000–3,000 kr/year)
  • Design tools — Adobe Creative Cloud or Canva Pro (~3,000 kr/year)
  • NFC chips — cost of inlays embedded per card
  • Payment processing — Stripe/Klarna fees (~1.5–2.5% per transaction)

Revenue Streams

  • Card sales — per-unit pricing with quantity discounts (est. ~80 kr/card at low qty, ~50 kr/card at 100+)
  • NFC setup fee — one-time fee for programming and linking chip to digital profile
  • vCard hosting subscription — annual recurring fee: Bas 99 kr/år (editable profile + private NFC UUID link) · Standard 119 kr/år (+ shareable personal link domain.com/name). År 1 ingår gratis vid kortköp. Fast infrastrukturkostnad ~100 kr/mån oavsett antal kunder.
  • Design/artwork service — optional premium design fee for clients without ready-to-print files
  • Corporate volume contracts — annual supply agreements with hotels and larger companies
